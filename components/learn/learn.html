🧠 What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity protects systems, networks, and data from attacks.

Example: Preventing hackers from stealing your password.

🌐 Network Basics

Internet is a system of connected devices.

Routers and servers manage communication.

🔐 Encryption

Encryption hides your data from attackers.

Example: hello → h3#kL9

🛡️ CIA Triad

Confidentiality: Only authorized users access data.

Integrity: Data remains unchanged.

Availability: Systems stay accessible.
